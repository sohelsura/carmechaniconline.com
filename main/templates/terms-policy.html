What is Roadside Assistance : -

Roadside Assistance (RSA) cover offers the much-needed relief to a car owner if his/her vehicle breaks down or has a mechanical failure on the road or at home. It can be very frustrating to be stranded in an unfamiliar place with no access to a mechanic. This is where the RSA cover comes in handy.

Benefits Proposed:-

We give below the definition and description of the various advantages that can be offered to customers in different combinations that can be agreed upon.

  • Flat Tyre Replacement:-
    If the Covered Vehicle has a punctured or burst tyre, carmechaniconline.com shall take care of changing it with the spare tyre carried in the Customer’s/Policyholder’s vehicle.
    We ensure that your vehicle’s flat tyre is repaired or replaced right away on-site so that you can get back on the road in as little time as possible. Our authorized team will reach your location in less than 20-25 Minutes, depending on the location, to fix the punctured tyre or replace it with a spare tyre.
